Common

(2020)
Editorial/Identity Design, Branding, Photography
170*240mm
<Common> is a lifestyle magazine for all women that does not discriminate women according to their stance but deals with realistic stories that we are facing in everyday lives through various contents including interviews, columns, and essays. The magazine was produced by four editors who met at the film festival and published through Tumblebug crowdfunding.
<Common> is currently stocked in ‘Egg Studio in Whistle Stop’ in Iksan, ‘The Pollack’, ‘Ghost Books’ in Daegu, ‘Bookstore Ujoosonyeon(Space boy)’ in Yongin, and ‘Storagebookandfilm Gangnam’, ‘House Books’, ‘Classic Bookstore’, ‘Nowafter Books’ in Seoul.
