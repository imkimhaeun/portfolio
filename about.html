Kim Haeun is an explorer, collector, and translator. She usually spends time exploring around, collecting something interesting, and then translating them into graphic-language. She mainly works in a wide range of graphic design dealing with printed materials, websites, photography, and videography. She is now majoring in Visual Communication Design at Hongik University, Korea. If you have any inquiries, please do not hesitate to get in contact for further information.
