Cinematic Seoul

(2020)
Web Design, Photography
1439*971px
<Cinematic Seoul> is a project to archive film locations in Seoul. Although one film was made as fiction, it also works as a documentary about space. It reveals what has changed or hasn’t changed since the time when the film was been shot. The exploration uncovered the hidden layer of Seoul while using films as ‘clues’. This project was completed in a form of a website including photos and essays that shows the contrast between the past and the present.
