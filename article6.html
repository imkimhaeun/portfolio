Tadao Ando

(2020)
Graphic/Editorial Design, Photography
297*420mm(Poster), 180*210mm(Booklet)
Editorial work for a Japanese architect ‘Tadao Ando’. Research was conducted in two ways: secondary research with a film <Ando Tadao(2016)> and field study at JCC creative center in Hyehwa, Seoul. A large number of images were photographed during the field study. The final work was completed in a 16 pages booklet.
