Minimal, Maximal

(2020)
Graphic Design

A series of posters for an exhibition <Minimal, Maximal(2002)>. It was a re-design project for the previous exhibition. Participants tried to embody the essential, fundamental concepts of both ‘Minimal’ and ‘Maximal’.
