Themis’ Scale

(2020)
Object/Graphic Design
500*200*390mm(Object), 80*150mm(Printed Stuff)
<Themis’ Scale> is a participation work in the exhibition <Infection and Immunity>, which was held at the Doosung Paper Gallery in Seoul from November 5 to 26, 2020. In an exhibition on social infection and tools to immunize individuals from it, the work defined one of the forms of social infection as ‘consumption infection’. <Themis’ Scale> was produced as a means of protecting individuals from a capitalistic(consumer) society.
It is an object that stamps on the stigma on the floor with one side of scale down when consumption exceeds a user-defined limit. Balance/imbalance of the scale and therefore motility were used as the main motif.
